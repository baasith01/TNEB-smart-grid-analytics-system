# TNEB Smart Grid Analytics Business Recommendations

## 1. High Demand Regions

Increase transformer capacity in high consumption districts.

## 2. Predictive Maintenance

Prioritize transformers with health score below 70.

## 3. Weather Based Planning

Increase preparedness during high temperature seasons.

## 4. Smart Energy Awareness

Encourage residential consumers to shift usage to off-peak hours.

## 5. Future Demand Planning

Use forecasting models to proactively allocate infrastructure resources.
